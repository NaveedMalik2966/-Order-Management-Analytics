# Order-Management-Analytics Dashboard
Business Insights Dashboard in Looker Studio

🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes end-to-end order management performance using historical sales data to uncover regional demand patterns, customer behavior, category contribution, and order trends over time.
The objective is not just visualization, but to support business decisions related to sales strategy, regional focus, customer segmentation, and operational planning.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights that a sales manager, operations lead, or business analyst can immediately use.

🎯 Business Questions Answered

Which regions and states contribute the highest order volumes?

How do customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) differ in order behavior?

Which product categories drive most of the demand?

Are orders growing or declining over time (yearly & monthly trends)?

Which cities are key demand hubs?

How evenly distributed are orders across customers?

📈 Key Insights & Findings

Regional Concentration: A small number of regions account for a disproportionately high share of total orders, indicating opportunities for focused regional strategies.

Segment Performance: Consumer customers dominate order volume, while Corporate and Home Office segments show more stable but smaller demand.

Category Dependency: One product category contributes the majority of orders, highlighting potential over-reliance and diversification opportunities.

Growth Trend: Orders show a clear upward trend over years, suggesting business expansion and increasing market penetration.

Seasonality: Monthly analysis reveals order spikes in specific months, useful for inventory planning and staffing decisions.

City-Level Insights: Major metropolitan cities generate significantly higher orders, supporting targeted city-level marketing.
